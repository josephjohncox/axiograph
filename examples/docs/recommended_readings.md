# Recommended Readings for Machining Knowledge

## Domain: Machining

- **Machinery's Handbook** by Erik Oberg (2020)
  Topics: speeds, feeds, materials, tolerances
  Importance: critical
  Notes: The definitive reference for all machining parameters. Every shop should have one.

- **Metal Cutting Principles** by Milton C. Shaw (2005)
  Topics: chip formation, cutting forces, tool wear, heat generation
  Importance: high
  Notes: Theoretical foundation for understanding cutting mechanics. Essential for process optimization.

- **Manufacturing Engineering and Technology** by Serope Kalpakjian (2013)
  Topics: manufacturing processes, materials science, design for manufacturing
  Importance: high
  Notes: Comprehensive textbook covering all manufacturing processes.

## Domain: Tool Wear

- **Fundamentals of Metal Cutting and Machine Tools** by B.L. Juneja (2003)
  Topics: tool wear mechanisms, tool life, cutting fluids
  Importance: medium
  Notes: Good coverage of wear mechanisms including diffusion, abrasion, and adhesion.

- **Metal Cutting Theory and Practice** by David A. Stephenson (2016)
  Topics: cutting mechanics, tool wear, process planning
  Importance: high
  Notes: Modern treatment with practical examples.

## Domain: Chatter and Vibration

- **Manufacturing Automation** by Yusuf Altintas (2012)
  Topics: chatter, stability lobes, process damping, spindle dynamics
  Importance: critical
  Notes: The definitive text on machining dynamics and chatter stability.

- **Theory of Self-Excited Machine Tool Chatter** by S.A. Tobias (1965)
  Topics: regenerative chatter, stability theory, vibration
  Importance: high
  Notes: Classic foundational work on chatter theory.

## Domain: Materials

- **ASM Handbook Volume 16: Machining** by ASM International (1989)
  Topics: material machinability, cutting parameters, tool selection
  Importance: high
  Notes: Comprehensive data on machining different materials.

- **Machining Data Handbook** by Metcut Research Associates (1980)
  Topics: speeds, feeds, machinability ratings
  Importance: medium
  Notes: Empirical data from extensive testing. Some data dated but principles apply.

## Domain: Process Planning

- **Computer-Aided Process Planning** by Chao-Hwa Chang (1998)
  Topics: CAPP, feature recognition, process selection
  Importance: medium
  Notes: Academic treatment of automated process planning.

- **Fundamentals of Modern Manufacturing** by Mikell P. Groover (2020)
  Topics: manufacturing systems, automation, process planning
  Importance: high
  Notes: Good overview of modern manufacturing concepts.
